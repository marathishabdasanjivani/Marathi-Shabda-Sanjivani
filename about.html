About Us

Our Mission

Marathi Shabda Sanjivani is a dedicated digital initiative aimed at the preservation, exploration, and celebration of the Marathi language. We believe that every word holds a story, a history, and a nuance that deserves to be cherished.

What We Do

Our platform serves as an accessible digital dictionary and educational resource. We curate meaningful Marathi vocabulary, complete with definitions, examples, and grammatical insights, to help students, language enthusiasts, and curious minds deepen their connection with the language.

The Creator

Marathi Shabda Sanjivani was founded by Aryan Ramteke, a passionate linguist and creative writer. Inspired by the depth of Marathi literature and the beauty of words, this project is a bridge between tradition and the digital future, designed to make language learning intuitive and engaging.

Contact Us

We are always looking to improve and expand our collection. If you have any suggestions, feedback, or inquiries, please feel free to reach out to us at marathi.shabda.sanjivani@gmail.com.
